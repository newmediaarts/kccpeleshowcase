How to...

Prepare your files

  • ▲ Create a folder with your files to submit
  • ▲ Name your files accordingly
    • ▲ eg. #category-agency-title-of-work-#.jpg,
      #s12-Melissa-Lum-Bricked-01.jpg

Notes:

  • ▲ All entries must be submitted digitally via the online entry system. All categories, unless specified otherwise, will be allowed three (3) to five (5) digital uploads.
  • ▲ For Logo Design – An icon, symbol, or trademark designed to represent a product, service, or organization. Entrant may submit a brief description of the client company and its purpose (max. 50 words) to help the judges understand the logo design objective.
    • ▲ Design the description document.
  • ▲ For Integrated Campaigns – Integrated Campaign entries may be accompanied by a written or digital (video) summary not to exceed 250 words (written) or 3 minutes or less (digital). Digital summaries must be submitted by uploading the video file during the online entry process.
  • ▲ See guidebook for more detailed information https://thesillyme.com/peleawards-guidebook
